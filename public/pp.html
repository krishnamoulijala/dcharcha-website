D'charcha Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 October 2025

Introduction Scope What We Collect Your Rights Contact

1. Introduction

Welcome to D'charcha, a civic-tech platform for visual storytelling, civic reporting, and community engagement. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal data responsibly and transparently.

This Privacy Policy explains how D'charcha Technologies Private Limited ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you use the D'charcha mobile application, website, or related services (the "Platform"). By using the Platform you consent to this policy and our Terms of Service. If you do not agree, please do not use the Platform.

2. Scope

  • All users of the D'charcha mobile app and website;
  • All civic submissions, AI-generated content, and community interactions;
  • Registered and guest users, including citizens, civic partners, and government collaborators.

3. What We Collect

3.1 Personal Information

  • Name, email address, phone number (if provided)
  • Profile photo or display name (optional)
  • Location data (GPS or approximate)
  • Device & network information (IP address, device ID, OS, push tokens)

3.2 Civic & AI Content

  • Uploaded photos, videos, captions, and issue tags
  • AI-generated visuals and transformations
  • Location metadata associated with submissions
  • Comments, reactions, and reports

3.3 Usage Data

  • App interactions, session duration, feature usage
  • Submission counts, engagement metrics, referral activity
  • Device event logs and diagnostics

3.4 Children's Data

Users under 18 must obtain parental or guardian consent. We may collect year of birth to verify eligibility.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • Enable civic reporting, visualization, and collaboration
  • Generate anonymized dashboards, heatmaps, and civic insights
  • Improve user experience and app performance
  • Send important updates, alerts, and responses
  • Moderate content and ensure compliance
  • Support research and policy design for public benefit

5. AI-Generated Content

AI outputs are illustrative and not official government plans. Such outputs may be stored, labeled, and analyzed for civic insight. Do not treat AI-generated visuals as factual or authoritative.

6. Data Sharing & Disclosure

  • With government or civic bodies to resolve reported issues;
  • With trusted third-party service providers (e.g., OpenAI, Firebase, Google Maps) who are contractually bound;
  • With law enforcement or regulators when legally required;
  • In aggregated/anonymized form for research, analytics, or public reporting.

7. Data Storage & Security

We store data on secure cloud infrastructure located in India. We use encryption in transit and at rest, access controls, and regular security assessments. Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel.

IP addresses are used only for approximate location and moderation and are not retained for personal identification.

8. Device Permissions

  • Camera – to capture or upload civic issues;
  • Location – to tag submissions and improve mapping accuracy;
  • Storage – to upload or save media;
  • Microphone – only if voice features are enabled.

Permissions are optional and can be managed via device settings.

9. Cookies & Tracking

We use limited session cookies on the web portal for navigation and performance. Cookies do not store personal data and expire after each session.

10. Your Rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you;
  • Correct or update inaccurate information;
  • Request deletion of your account and personal data;
  • Withdraw consent for processing;
  • Request a copy of your data in a readable format.

To exercise these rights, contact support@dcharcha.in or use the in-app settings.

11. Data Retention

We retain personal data while your account is active and as needed for legal compliance, dispute resolution, and fraud prevention. Aggregated and anonymized data may be retained indefinitely for research and civic analytics.

Upon a deletion request, we will erase or anonymize personal data within 30 working days, subject to legal exceptions.

12. Compliance & Legal Obligations

We comply with Indian laws including the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, the Information Technology Act, 2000, and related rules. We may disclose data to comply with legal requests, court orders, or to protect public safety.
